About Me

Collaborating with world-class experts in UX

I launched this website to help the growing number of Internet users who now days use for their work not only PCs but also laptops, mobile phones and smart phones like iPhone, tablet devices like iPad and many others. The screen resolution of all those devices ranges from 320px to 1024px or more. Therefore, the old websites that have a fixed width are no longer working. And we need a responsive website design to correctly display all websites on most of devices. So I launched Free Responsive Templates to help you to create an automatically adapted web site for all screen resolutions and devices.
